Who We Are

A to Z supply chain
partner for industry.

GISS is a premier industrial trading company in India, dedicated to supplying high-quality raw materials, refractory items, alloys and master alloys, soda ash, insulation materials, pig iron, steel items and equipment to the manufacturing sector. We bridge the gap between global suppliers and local industries, delivering certified products with stringent quality assurance and punctual delivery at a very competitive price.

Founded on the principles of reliability and efficiency, GISS serves as a comprehensive "A to Z" supply chain partner for industrial operations worldwide. We understand the rigorous demands of modern facilities, which is why our product portfolio consists of rigorously tested materials designed to withstand heavy-duty use — built on 25 years of expertise and global partnership.
